# Functional Requirements / User Stories: CountIt [Jewellery]

## 1. Tenant & RBAC

### 1.1 Tenant Registration
- **As a** new tenant owner, **I want to** register my business and auto-create a tenant DB, **so that** I can start using the system.
- **Acceptance Criteria**:
  - User can register with email, business name, and basic details.
  - A new tenant DB is created automatically.
  - The registrant is assigned the "Tenant Owner" role by default.

### 1.2 Tenant Admin Invitation
- **As a** tenant owner, **I want to** invite multiple tenant admins, **so that** they can manage branches and users.
- **Acceptance Criteria**:
  - Tenant owner can invite users via email.
  - Invited users receive a link to join and set up their account.
  - Joined users are assigned the "Tenant Admin" role.

### 1.3 RBAC & Permissions
- **As a** tenant admin, **I want to** assign roles and permissions to users, **so that** access is controlled per feature.
- **Acceptance Criteria**:
  - Roles include: Tenant Owner, Tenant Admin, Branch Admin, Seller, Accountant, etc.
  - Permissions are configurable per role (e.g., view products, edit prices, approve stock transfers).
  - Users can be assigned to specific branches.

## 2. Product & Inventory

### 2.1 Product Creation
- **As a** product manager, **I want to** create products with SKU, multiple units, and base conversion, **so that** I can manage inventory in different units.
- **Acceptance Criteria**:
  - Product has a unique SKU.
  - Multiple units can be defined (e.g., gram, piece, set) with conversion factors to a base unit.
  - Product can be marked as taxable/non-taxable, discountable/non-discountable.
  - Product can have attributes (e.g., metal type, gemstone).

### 2.2 Child and Add-on Products
- **As a** product manager, **I want to** define child products and add-on products, **so that** they are auto-included or suggested during sales.
- **Acceptance Criteria**:
  - Child products are auto-included when the parent product is sold.
  - Add-on products are suggested during sales and can be selected optionally.

### 2.3 Vendor and Customer-Type Rates
- **As a** product manager, **I want to** set vendor rates and customer-type rates, **so that** pricing varies by vendor and customer type.
- **Acceptance Criteria**:
  - Vendor rates per product with different units and attributes.
  - Customer-type rates per product.
  - Outlet-specific rates per product.

### 2.4 Batch-Based Inventory
- **As a** inventory manager, **I want to** track stock by SKU, batch, and outlet, **so that** I have accurate inventory visibility.
- **Acceptance Criteria**:
  - Opening stock can be added with batch.
  - Purchases create or update batches.
  - Sales consume batches.
  - Transfers, adjustments, and consumption update batches with approval flows.
  - Stock is queryable by SKU, batch, and outlet.

## 3. POS & Sales

### 3.1 Sales Transaction
- **As a** seller, **I want to** process sales with batch selection, tax, and discounts, **so that** I can complete customer transactions accurately.
- **Acceptance Criteria**:
  - Seller selects products and batches.
  - System applies tax based on product tax category.
  - Discounts are applied if product is discountable.
  - Total amount is calculated correctly.
  - Stock is updated in real-time.

### 3.2 Payment Methods
- **As a** seller, **I want to** accept payments via Cash, Cheque, Online Transfer, QR, **so that** customers have flexible payment options.
- **Acceptance Criteria**:
  - Payment method is recorded per sale.
  - Receipt is generated with payment details.

### 3.3 Customer Payments with TDS and Additional Headings
- **As a** finance user, **I want to** record customer payments with TDS and additional headings, **so that** accounting records are accurate.
- **Acceptance Criteria**:
  - TDS is calculated and recorded.
  - Additional headings (additions/deductions by amount or percent) are applied to gross, taxable, or discount amounts.

## 4. Purchases & Suppliers

### 4.1 Purchase with Batch and VAT
- **As a** purchase manager, **I want to** record purchases with batch IDs and VAT flags, **so that** I can track inventory and tax compliance.
- **Acceptance Criteria**:
  - Purchase creates or updates batches.
  - Invoice can be tagged as VAT/non-VAT.
  - Claimable VAT percentage is recorded if applicable.

### 4.2 Supplier Payments with TDS and Additional Headings
- **As a** finance user, **I want to** make supplier payments with TDS and additional headings, **so that** accounting records are accurate.
- **Acceptance Criteria**:
  - TDS is calculated and recorded.
  - Additional headings are applied as configured.

## 5. Tax, Discounts, Accounting

### 5.1 Tax Configuration
- **As a** admin, **I want to** configure tax categories and rules, **so that** sales and purchases are tax-compliant.
- **Acceptance Criteria**:
  - Tax categories (e.g., VAT 13%) can be created.
  - Products are assigned to tax categories.
  - Tax is applied correctly in sales and purchases.

### 5.2 Accounting Ledger Mapping
- **As a** admin, **I want to** map customers, suppliers, employees to accounting ledgers, **so that** inventory and accounting modules are linked.
- **Acceptance Criteria**:
  - Each customer, supplier, employee has a linked ledger.
  - Inventory transactions update corresponding ledgers based on configuration.

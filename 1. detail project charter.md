# Project Charter: CountIt [Jewellery]

## 1. Project Overview
- **Project Name**: CountIt [Jewellery]
- **Project Type**: Multi-tenant SaaS Inventory & POS System for Jewelry Businesses
- **Platforms**: 
  - Backend: .NET Web API
  - Frontend: React Native Mobile App
- **Key Capabilities**:
  - Multi-tenant architecture with separate tenant databases
  - Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) with configurable permissions
  - Multi-branch/outlet management
  - Multi-currency and multi-unit product management
  - Batch-based inventory tracking (SKU + batch + outlet)
  - POS with tax, discounts, and multiple payment methods
  - Purchase, sales, stock transfer, adjustment, and consumption workflows
  - Customer and supplier payments with TDS and additional headings
  - Configurable linkage to accounting modules via ledger mapping

## 2. Business Objectives
- Provide jewelry businesses with a centralized, scalable system to manage inventory across multiple branches.
- Enable real-time stock visibility by SKU, batch, and outlet.
- Support tax-compliant invoicing (VAT/non-VAT, claimable VAT).
- Integrate inventory transactions with accounting systems via ledger mapping.
- Scale securely to multiple tenants with isolated data and configurable user roles.

## 3. High-Level Scope
### 3.1 In Scope
- **Tenant & RBAC**
  - Tenant registration and automatic tenant DB creation
  - Tenant owner and admin roles
  - RBAC with permission list
  - Invitation and onboarding of tenant admins
- **Product & Inventory**
  - Products with unique SKU, multiple units, and base unit conversion
  - Child products and add-on products
  - Vendor rates and product attributes
  - Customer-type rates and outlet-specific rates
  - Batch-based inventory (purchase, sales, transfer, adjustment, consumption)
  - Stock tracking by SKU, batch, and outlet
- **POS & Sales**
  - Multi-branch POS terminals
  - Sales with batch selection, tax, discounts
  - Payment methods: Cash, Cheque, Online Transfer, QR
  - Customer payment receipts with TDS and additional headings
- **Purchases & Suppliers**
  - Purchase with/without batch, VAT/non-VAT, claimable VAT
  - Supplier payments with TDS and additional headings
- **Tax, Discounts, and Accounting Linkage**
  - Tax categories and rules (e.g., VAT 13%)
  - Discountable/non-discountable products
  - Price change permissions for sellers
  - Accounting ledger mapping for customers, suppliers, employees
  - Configurable inventory ↔ accounting module linkage

### 3.2 Out of Scope (Examples)
- Advanced analytics and predictive reporting (phase 2)
- Full-featured accounting module beyond ledger mapping (phase 2)
- Complex third-party integrations (e.g., government tax portals)
- Web-based admin panel (if only mobile is in scope)

## 4. Key Stakeholders
- **Client**: [Client Name/Department]
- **End Users**: Tenant owners, branch admins, sellers, accountants

## 5. High-Level Timeline & Milestones
- **Phase 1**: Core multi-tenant setup, product/inventory, basic POS
- **Phase 2**: Advanced POS, tax/discounts, payments
- **Phase 3**: Accounting linkage, reporting, UAT, Go-Live

## 6. Success Criteria
- UAT sign-off from client
- Go-live with at least [X] tenants
- Key metrics: reduced stock discrepancies, faster checkout times, compliant tax reporting

## 7. High-Level Risks
- Complexity of multi-tenant DB design and performance
- Complex tax and discount rules
- RBAC and permission management overhead
- Integration with accounting systems

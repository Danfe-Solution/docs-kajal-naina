## 3. Notes on Nepal-Specific Accounting Rules

- **Fiscal Year**: Nepal’s fiscal year runs from Shrawan 1 to Ashad 31 (mid-July to mid-July). Use Nepali year notation like `20822083`.
- **Chart of Accounts**: Follow a standard structure:
  - 1000–1999: Assets
  - 2000–2999: Liabilities
  - 3000–3999: Income
  - 4000–4999: Expenses
- **Voucher Numbering**: Prefix with voucher type and fiscal year (e.g., `JV/208283/0001`).
- **Tax Compliance**: Ensure ledgers are tagged appropriately for VAT, TDS, and income tax reporting.

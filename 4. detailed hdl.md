# High-Level Design: CountIt [Jewellery]

## 1. System Architecture
- **Backend**: .NET Web API (RESTful)
- **Frontend**: React Native mobile app
- **Databases**:
  - Master DB: Tenants, Users, Subscriptions, RBAC mappings
  - Tenant DBs (one per tenant): Products, Inventory, Branches, Transactions, Ledgers, etc.
- **Key Components**:
  - Authentication & Authorization Service
  - Tenant Management Service
  - Product & Inventory Service
  - POS & Sales Service
  - Purchase & Supplier Service
  - Tax & Discount Engine
  - Accounting Linkage Service

## 2. Data Flow Highlights

### 2.1 Tenant Onboarding
1. User registers via frontend.
2. Backend creates tenant record in Master DB.
3. Tenant Management Service creates a new tenant DB.
4. RBAC setup: registrant becomes Tenant Owner.

### 2.2 Product & Inventory Flow
1. Product manager creates products with units, attributes, vendor rates, etc.
2. Purchases create batches and update stock.
3. Sales consume batches and update stock.
4. Transfers, adjustments, and consumption update batches with approval flows.

### 2.3 POS Flow
1. Seller logs in and selects branch/terminal.
2. Seller adds products and batches to cart.
3. System applies tax and discounts.
4. Seller selects payment method and completes sale.
5. Stock and ledgers are updated.

## 3. Security & RBAC
- JWT-based authentication.
- Role-based permissions (tenant owner, admin, branch admin, seller, etc.).
- Tenant isolation via separate DBs.
- Permission checks on all sensitive endpoints.

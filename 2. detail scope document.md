# Scope Document: CountIt [Jewellery]

## 1. In-Scope Features

### 1.1 Multi-Tenant & RBAC
- Tenant registration and automatic tenant DB creation
- Tenant owner role (default for registrant)
- Invitation and onboarding of multiple tenant admins
- RBAC with configurable permission list
- User role assignments per tenant and branch

### 1.2 Product & Inventory
- Products with unique SKU, name, description
- Multiple units per product with base unit conversion
- Child products (auto-included) and add-on products (optional)
- Vendor rates per product with different units and attributes
- Product attributes (e.g., metal type, gemstone, weight)
- Customer-type rates and outlet-specific rates
- Batch-based inventory:
  - Opening stock with batch
  - Purchase with batch ID (auto-generated if not provided)
  - Sales with batch selection
  - Consumption with batch and approval flow
  - New batch creation with approval flow
  - Stock adjustment with batch and approval flow
  - Stock transfer between outlets with approval flow
- Stock tracking by SKU, batch, and outlet for each transaction

### 1.3 POS & Sales
- Multi-branch POS terminals
- Sales transactions with:
  - Product and batch selection
  - Tax application based on product tax category
  - Discounts (if product is discountable)
  - Payment methods: Cash, Cheque, Online Transfer, QR
- Customer payment receipts with:
  - TDS
  - Additional headings (additions/deductions by amount or percent of gross, taxable, discount)

### 1.4 Purchases & Suppliers
- Purchase transactions with:
  - Batch ID (auto-generated if not provided)
  - VAT/non-VAT invoice tagging
  - Claimable VAT percentage
- Supplier payments with:
  - TDS
  - Additional headings (additions/deductions by amount or percent of gross, taxable, discount)

### 1.5 Tax, Discounts, and Accounting Linkage
- Tax categories and rules (e.g., VAT 13% applied to taxable amount)
- Product tags for:
  - Taxable/non-taxable
  - Discountable/non-discountable
  - Price change permission for sellers
- Purchase invoice tags for:
  - VAT/non-VAT
  - Claimable VAT percentage
- Accounting ledger mapping for:
  - Customers
  - Suppliers
  - Employees
- Configurable inventory and accounting module linkage

## 2. Out-of-Scope Features (Examples)
- Advanced analytics and BI dashboards (phase 2)
- Full-featured accounting module beyond ledger mapping (phase 2)
- Third-party ERP/accounting system integrations beyond basic ledger mapping (phase 2)
- Web-based admin panel (if only mobile is in scope)

## 3. Change Control Process
- All scope changes must be documented in a Change Request.
- Impact analysis (timeline, cost, effort) must be approved by [Client Representative].
- Approved changes will be tracked in a Change Log.
